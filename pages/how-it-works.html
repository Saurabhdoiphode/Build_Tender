How BuildTender Works

A step-by-step guide to transforming your construction project from start to finish.

Get Started Learn More

The Complete 20-Step Process

01
House Owner Registration
House owner registers with basic details and creates an account.
  • Submit personal information
  • Email verification
  • Login to dashboard
02
Tender Request Submission
Submit detailed project requirements including scope, timeline, and specifications.
  • Project description
  • Upload building plans
  • Specify requirements
03
Verify Admin Review
Our verification team checks all documents and project requirements for completeness.
  • Document verification
  • Requirement analysis
  • Approval or correction
04
Budget Estimation
Budget Admin prepares detailed cost estimation within 24 hours.
  • Material cost analysis
  • Labor estimation
  • Detailed breakdown
05
Budget Verification
Verify Admin reviews and approves the budget estimate.
  • Budget review
  • Adjustments if needed
  • Final approval
06
House Owner Approval
House owner reviews and approves the budget to proceed.
  • Review estimates
  • Confirm readiness
  • Proceed to next step
07-08
Tender Upload & Partner Verification
Tender is posted on platform while contractors and vendors are verified.
  • Tender goes live
  • Partner verification
  • Contractor bidding opens
09
Contractor Participation
Verified contractors review tender and submit competitive bids.
  • Review project scope
  • Submit detailed bids
  • Include timeline
10
Contractor Selection
House owner compares and selects the best contractor based on multiple criteria.
  • Compare bids
  • Check ratings
  • Make selection
11
Agreement & Bond Verification
Contractors submit security bonds and agreements for verification.
  • Submit bonds
  • Agreement signing
  • Admin verification
12
Payment Initiation
Payment process begins with milestone-based disbursement tracking.
  • Initial payment
  • Milestone tracking
  • Payment approval
13-14
Construction & Quality Inspections
Construction begins with regular quality check inspections on site.
  • Work initiation
  • Regular inspections
  • Quality reports
15
Progress Monitoring
Track real-time project progress and identify any delays immediately.
  • Update tracking
  • Flag delays
  • Coordinate adjustments
16
Material & Service Management
Vendors deliver materials and service providers complete assigned tasks.
  • Materials delivery
  • Service coordination
  • Logging activities
17
Issue Resolution
Any issues are recorded and resolved quickly with admin coordination.
  • Report issues
  • Coordinate solutions
  • Track resolution
18
Final Inspection
Comprehensive final quality inspection and completion verification.
  • Quality verification
  • Scope verification
  • Completion report
19-20
Payment & Feedback
Final payment release and project feedback/rating collection.
  • Final payment
  • Project closure
  • Ratings & feedback

Our Verification Process

Every participant in BuildTender goes through strict verification to ensure trust and quality.

For House Owners

Identity verification and account validation to ensure genuine users.

For Contractors

Comprehensive verification including licenses, certifications, and project portfolio.

For Vendors

Business registration, GST details, and product quality verification.
